ABOUT US

Founded in 1999 by passionate athletes, who won numerous awards and medals for their high-level performances, Magnus and Malene teamed up to start the Catharsis Park and share their passion and skills with their fans and all the people wanting to learn, while having fun at the same time. In 2009, the park grew and became so popular that another level of management needed to be implemented, and so Sophia came in the picture with her great managerial skills and became CEO of our park. When the park started offering high level archery activities, medalist Jean came to work with us. Our team of professionally trained instructors is looking forward to meeting you, and to initiate or perfect your skills.
